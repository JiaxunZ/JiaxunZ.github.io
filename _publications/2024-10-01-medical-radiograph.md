**Authors:** H Jiang, X Hao, Y Huang, C Ma, **Jiaxun Zhang**, R Zhang

This paper presents a hybrid pre-training paradigm for advancing medical radiograph representation learning. The approach leverages multilevel semantic granularity to improve the quality of learned representations for medical image analysis tasks.
