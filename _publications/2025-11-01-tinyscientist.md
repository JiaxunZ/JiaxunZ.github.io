**Authors:** H Yu\*, K Xuan\*, F Li\*, K Zhu, Z Lei, **Jiaxun Zhang**, Z Qi, K Richardson, J You

*"\*" indicates equal contribution*

This paper introduces TinyScientist, an interactive, extensible, and controllable framework for building research agents. The system enables researchers to create AI agents that can assist with various research tasks while maintaining user control and interpretability.

**Venue:** EMNLP 2025 (System Demonstrations)
